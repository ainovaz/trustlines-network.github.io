Mobile payments for everyone

The Trustlines Network comes as a mobile payment app with a unique onboarding experience.
Using it, is as easy as:

  • Downloading the app
  • Connecting with a friend
  • Start sending & receiving payments
Check out our prototype

It can be this open and easy because the system works without any central intermediators, as

  • the role of credit and money issuance is filled by regular users of the system, who maintain Trustlines with their friends.
  • the blockchain is used to facilitate secure payments and keep a verifiable record of all real world obligations.
